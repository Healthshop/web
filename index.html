Welcome to the pre-authorization request application for elective procedures. Please begin your pre-authorization request by searching for your condition or by uploading your las vist summary note from your physician.

At the end of this process, you will have scheduled an informational sesstion to learn more about your preferred care provider. We will be informed by your informational advisor that your session has completed. We will then complete the rest of the pre-authorization.

Same-day informationals are available for urgent cases. In most situations, we can speak with you within a 48 to 72 hour timeframe.
